# Appendix C. Terminology Notes

## Goal

What the work is actually for.

## Constraint

What may not be sacrificed while doing the work.

## Definition of done

The conditions that must be true before the task is considered complete.

## Scope

The area the model is allowed or expected to inspect.

## Boundary

The line beyond which the task should not spread.

## Non-goal

Something explicitly excluded from the current task or version.

## Verification

The evidence or checks that confirm the answer is good enough.

## Change axis

A dimension along which the system is likely to evolve in the future.
