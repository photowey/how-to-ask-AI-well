## What This Book Argues

Most people think they have an AI problem when they really have a **question-design problem**. They ask vaguely, scope loosely, verify weakly, and then blame the model for producing shallow work. This book argues that asking well is not a minor communication trick. It is a working method.

## What You Will Get

- A readable explanation of why so many AI conversations fail
- A practical model for structuring strong requests (8 modules)
- Concrete templates you can reuse
- Anti-patterns that reliably produce shallow work
- Applied playbooks for debugging, refactoring, design, review, writing, and research
- Part V (placeholder): hands-on labs and case studies to come
